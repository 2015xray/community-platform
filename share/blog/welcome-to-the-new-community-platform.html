The community platform has been given a fresh new look, and we'd like your help to keep making it better.

Extending This Platform

Care was taken to make this platform accessible and predictable when adding new features. Want to add a button? <a class="button"> is all it takes! Need it to be blue? That's just <a class="button blue">.

Need a two column layout? <div class="half">Column1></div><div class="half">Column2<div> - no crazy grid wrappers needed, because floats are cleared at a .row level. This "simple grid" was built to allow contributors to build out the platform without worrying about yet another syntax or trying to remember obscure grid classes and rules.

There's obviously still a lot going on and a lot more examples we could give, but we're confident that this platform will be easy for new and experienced users to comprehend and extend as needed.

A Friendly Translation Platform

A key goal in this process was to make the experience of translating DuckDuckGo into your langauge a little more enjoyable. There's still a lot of work to do to make DuckDuckGo more globally accessible, so we'd like to take this opportunity to ask for your help in translating DuckDuckGo into your language.
